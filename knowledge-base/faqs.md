# VoiceAI — Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Billing

### How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your registered email address. The link expires in 24 hours.

### How do I update my billing information?
Go to Settings > Billing in your dashboard. Click "Update Payment Method" to add or change your credit card. You can also update your billing address there.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Contact support within 30 days of your first payment for a full refund.

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard. Click on any order to see real-time tracking information. You'll also receive email updates at each shipping milestone.

### What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Initiate a return from "My Orders" > select order > "Request Return".

### How long does shipping take?
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days
- Overnight shipping: Next business day

### My order arrived damaged. What should I do?
Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund within 24 hours.

## Technical Support

### The app is not loading. What should I do?
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions
5. If the issue persists, contact support

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?
Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. You can use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) or receive SMS codes.

### What browsers are supported?
We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.
