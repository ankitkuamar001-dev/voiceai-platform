# VoiceAI — Company Policies

## Customer Service Policy

### Response Time Guarantees
| Priority | First Response | Resolution |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| Critical (P1) | 5 minutes | 1 hour |
| High (P2) | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| Medium (P3) | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| Low (P4) | 4 hours | 24 hours |

### Escalation Policy
- **Level 1**: AI Voice Agent handles initial contact
- **Level 2**: Human agent takes over if AI confidence < 70% or customer requests
- **Level 3**: Supervisor involvement for complaints or unresolved issues > 30 minutes
- **Level 4**: Manager escalation for legal, safety, or high-value customer issues

### Refund Policy
- Full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked
- Partial refund (50%) between 31-60 days
- Store credit only after 60 days
- Damaged/defective items: full refund at any time
- Digital products: refund within 14 days if unused

### Data Privacy
- All conversations are recorded for quality assurance
- Recordings are stored encrypted for 90 days
- Customers can request conversation deletion (GDPR right to erasure)
- PII is automatically redacted from transcripts
- No customer data is shared with third parties

### Agent Conduct
- Always greet the customer by name when available
- Confirm important actions before executing them
- Never make promises the company cannot keep
- Escalate immediately if customer mentions legal action
- Document all interactions in the ticket system
